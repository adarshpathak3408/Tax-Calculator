How It Works?

The tax calculation works based on this formula:-


Overall income = Gross Annual Income + Extra Income - Deductions



Overall Income (after deductions) under 8 (≤) Lakhs is not taxed


Income over 8 (>) Lakhs, the amount over 8 Lakhs is taxed at:-

  • 30% for people with age < 40
  • 40% for people with age ≥ 40 but < 60
  • 10% for people with age ≥ 60
Gross annual income is your total salary in a year before any deduction

Please enter a valid number

Extra income is the income that you earn from other sources

Please enter a valid number

Age group is to determine the tax according to the age
Deductions reduce taxable income by subtracting expenses
